No installation needed! The app is ready to use immediately in your web browser.

---

## ⚡ What You Can Do

### 1️⃣ **Search Weather**
- Enter a city name: London, New York, Tokyo, Sydney, Paris, Mumbai, Dubai, or Berlin
- Click "Search" or press Enter
- View detailed weather information including:
  - Current temperature
  - Feels like temperature
  - Humidity percentage
  - Wind speed
  - Atmospheric pressure
  - Visibility
  - Cloud coverage

### 2️⃣ **View Search History**
- Click the "History" tab
- See all your previous weather searches
- Each entry shows city, temperature, condition, and timestamp
- History is automatically saved in your browser
- Click "Clear History" to reset

### 3️⃣ **Compare Two Cities**
- Click the "Compare" tab
- Enter two city names
- Click "Compare"
- See side-by-side weather comparison
- Compare temperature, humidity, wind, pressure, and conditions

### 4️⃣ **Check Statistics**
- Click the "Statistics" tab
- View average temperature from your searches
- Check average humidity levels
- See total number of searches made

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**App not loading?**
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check your internet connection

**History not saving?**
- Ensure cookies/storage are enabled
- Check if in private/incognito mode (won't save in private mode)
- Try a different browser

**Weather not showing?**
- Make sure you spelled the city name correctly
- Use one of the 8 available demo cities
- Try lowercase letters
